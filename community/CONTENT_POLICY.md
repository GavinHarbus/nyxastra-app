# Community Template Content Policy

This policy applies to every template submitted to the NyxAstra
community gallery. We accept submissions from anyone, but we reserve
the right to reject or remove templates that violate the rules below.

---

## Not allowed

Templates that **prompt for**, **describe**, or **demonstrate**:

1. **Sexual content involving minors.** Zero tolerance. Reported to law
   enforcement where applicable.
2. **Sexually explicit imagery** of any subject. The gallery is
   work-safe.
3. **Hateful or harassing depictions** of individuals or groups based
   on race, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, etc.
4. **Real-world violence**, gore, or self-harm intended to shock.
5. **Identifiable real persons** (politicians, celebrities, private
   individuals) without clear satirical or fair-use framing.
6. **Content designed to deceive** — fake news imagery, forged
   documents, deepfake-style impersonation prompts.
7. **Trademarked logos or copyrighted character likenesses** as the
   primary subject (`a Disney Mickey Mouse poster`, etc.).
8. **Anything that violates the [OpenAI usage policies][openai-policy]**
   or the [Azure OpenAI Code of Conduct][azure-policy] — since templates
   ultimately call those APIs.

[openai-policy]: https://openai.com/policies/usage-policies/
[azure-policy]: https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/legal/cognitive-services/openai/code-of-conduct

---

## Use of variables to bypass the policy

Templates that pass the policy in their default form but are obviously
designed to be filled in with prohibited content (e.g. a portrait
template whose `{{subject}}` is hinted toward minors) will be rejected.
We look at the *full intent*, not just the surface text.

---

## Cover images

Cover images must be:

- **Generated by the template itself**, not pulled from external
  sources, stock libraries, or other artists' work.
- **Free of personally identifiable information** in EXIF metadata. CI
  strips EXIF on import, but please don't include identifying details
  in the image itself.
- **Work-safe.** No nudity, no graphic violence.

---

## Author identity

You may submit under:

- Your real name
- A handle / pseudonym
- `Anonymous`

Whichever you choose will be displayed in the gallery. We do not
collect or verify identity. **Don't impersonate someone else.**

---

## Reporting violations

If you find a template in the gallery that violates this policy, please
[open an issue][issue] with the `community-template-issue` label and
include the template slug. Maintainers respond within a few business
days.

[issue]: https://github.com/GavinHarbus/nyxastra-app/issues/new?template=template_issue.yml

---

## Changes to this policy

This document evolves. Significant changes are announced in the project
[CHANGELOG](../CHANGELOG.md). The version of the policy in effect for
any given template is the one that was current when the template was
merged.
